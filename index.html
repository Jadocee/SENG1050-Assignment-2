our services

Want the best looking car on the street? Your local Jim's Cars team can help, often with a same day service if our schedule permits. Whether its regular B&S, or a one-off swap, the team at Jim's Cars can help.

buy

Jim’s Cars offers an affordable CBLS – from initial car design, to add-on selection, mileage, performance, seating and more. So if you are looking to buy a new car, or just need a car for the weekend, or need a loan to buy that dream car, don’t put off the purchase any longer. Call us for a free quote on 131 546 or Book Online. Your local Jim can work with you to come up with your perfect car, and ensure that the result is practical, sustainable and easy to maintain.

swap

Need the car swapped? And don't have the time, tools or expertise to do it yourself? Give Jim's Cars a call on 131 546 - we can often provide a same-day car swap if our schedule permits. Whether you are after a one-off, quick swap before the next road-trip, a car to use while yours is being repaired, or more regular car swapping, let us show you why Jim’s is the largest and most trusted CSS in Australia.

sell

Putting off selling that car? Call Jim’s on 131 546 or Book Online – we can even have the car sold today if our schedule permits! We do thorough inspections and cleaning – often on the same day you sell – taking away rubbish and debris fast. We leave you with the cash from the sale. Sell the car and gain glorious garage space.
